White Water Rafting

What is White Water Rafting?

White water rafting is a recreational outdoor activity which uses an inflatable raft to navigate a river or other body of water. This is often done on white water or different degrees of rough water, in order to thrill and excite the raft passengers. The development of this activity as a leisure sport has become popular since the mid-1970s.
