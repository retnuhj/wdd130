I have earned a Bachelor of Science degree and a Master of Business Administration (MBA) from Southern Utah University (SUU) and a PhD in e-Learning from North Central University (NCU) in Prescott, Arizona. My dissertation focused on the delivery aspect on online courses.
I enjoy the challenge of working with students in higher education, particularly in online courses, trying to foster a sense of community where you may never 'see' your instructor or fellow classmates. I continue to research in the areas of assessment in online courses.
